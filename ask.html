Our dog, Sparky, likes to eat whatever the kids are snacking on. Is it OK for the dog to eat chocolate?
Chocolate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed your dogs chocolate. Try playing a game with your children — when you feed them people treats, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
